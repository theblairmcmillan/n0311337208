A BRIEF HISTORY

Built at a time when Downtown Nashville was experiencing a boom of new hotels and department stores on nearly every corner, Noelle boasts a rich history in Nashville’s storied past. Backing up to Nashville’s famed Printers Alley, and just a block from Broadway, Noelle has long-been at the center of the downtown hustle-and-bustle since it was built in 1930.

Now in its second act, Noelle has once again found its home among Nashville’s creative voices, playing host to some of Nashville’s finest makers, and a welcome companion to the spirit that makes this place so unique.
