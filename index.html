A STORIED PAST

Built in 1930 at a time when downtown Nashville was experiencing a boom of new hotels, and department stores were going up on nearly every corner, Noelle boasts a rich history. With the famed Printer’s Alley at her back and the glow of Broadway just a block away, Noelle has long been at the center of the hustle and bustle that fuels this colorful city.

Now in its second act, Noelle has risen once again to take on its role in Nashville’s rich narrative. Today, the famed hotel serves as a gathering place for some of the city’s most interesting voices and makers as it continues to contribute to the spirit that makes this place so unique.
